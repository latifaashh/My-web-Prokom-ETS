What is Lateef Indonesia?

Lateef Indonesia is one of the well-known brands in Indonesia that provides Muslim clothing with a variety of fashionable models. Lateef Indonesia was founded in 2020. The owner of Lateef Indonesia is Latifah Sekar, a successful young entrepreneur in the food and clothing business.
